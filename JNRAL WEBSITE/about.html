Profile

JNRAL FAMILY CORPORATION, formerly known as MANAS MEDICAL CLINIC is twenty two bed capacity health care facility situated along the course of A. Soriano Highway at Barangay Sahud-Ulan, Tanza, Cavite. A Pediatrician who is also a resident in the area established in it 1995 intentionally as an Outpatient and Emergency Clinic. The structure was simple as it caters mostly among the average and below average income strata of the locality. However, through the passing years, the Institution has generated a growing number of patients and the owner felt it was high time to upgrade it officially as a Birthing Home Clinic.

In August 2006, a group of Inspectors from the Department of Health came over to visit the place. The permit for the Birthing Home Clinic was not granted as they thought the facility would be more appropriate rather as an Infirmary Hospital. Hence, it was applied as a level 1 hospital and was officially given the license to operate in October 2007.

The Clinic is very optimistic and very promising to express its potential as Health Care Provider in the area not only because a Health and Business Consultant is manning it but also because of its necessity and demand.

Barangay Sahud-Ulan is only one of the 41 Barangays comprising the Municipality of Tanza, Cavite. Having a latest total Town population of 230,621 JRAL FAMILY CORPORATION, can be a potential major player in the local Hospital Industry.

JNRAL Family Corporation Hospital has been operating for about 29 years. It is being run by a Pediatrician and manned by four consultants - 9 Obstetrician, 7 Pediatricians and 7 Internist, 2 Ophthalmologist, 3 EENT, 6 Surgeon, 16 Anesthesiologists, 2 Dentist, 2 Ortho.. It has reputation of having a quality service, accessible and affordable clinic. There are 90 members of the staff.
